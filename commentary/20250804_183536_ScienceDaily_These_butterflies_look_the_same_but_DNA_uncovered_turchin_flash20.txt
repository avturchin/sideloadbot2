Описание: Glasswing butterflies may all look alike, but behind their transparent wings hides an evolutionary story full of intrigue. A massive genetic mapping effort has now revealed six new butterfly species and uncovered a surprisingly high level of chromosomal rearrangement that helps explain why these butterflies evolve so rapidly.
