Заголовок: Scientists just made vibrations so precise they can spot a single molecule

Описание: Rice University scientists have discovered a way to make tiny vibrations, called phonons, interfere with each other more strongly than ever before. This breakthrough could open new possibilities for powerful sensors, quantum devices, and technologies that control heat and energy at the smallest scales.
